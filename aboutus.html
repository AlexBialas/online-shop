About us

The Petshop is the leading specialist retailer of pet goods,accessories and aquatics in the Middle East. Founded in 2011 byCharlotte and Anders Jorgensen, the company grew from their smallvilla in Dubai into the largest retailer of pet food and supplies inthe region. With five stores around the UAE, including a54,000-square-foot Megastore in Dubai Investment Park, and an onlinestore offering a range of 12,000+ products, we deliver straight toyour doorstep! We offer same-day delivery in Dubai if you orderbefore 9 AM & free delivery all over UAE if your order is above AED100. The Petshop offers quality and convenience. All of which areavailable to you in just a few clicks. We are dedicated to bringingjoy to the lives of pets and their pet parents every single day.Apart from our extensive range of products we also offer a widerange of services to help support you on your journey as a lovingpet parent. These include pet relocation services as well as mobileand in-store grooming for cats and dogs.
